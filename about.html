A bit about myself.

I’m Maggie — an artist, designer, and first-year student at Rhode Island School of Design. Currently planning to major in Graphic Design, I’m based between Providence, Rhode Island and my home city of Auckland, New Zealand.


I’m always interested in:

  • exploring new avenues in my creative practice 🌱
  • learning new languages (both human and code!) 🤖
  • blurring the boundary between art and STEM 🖍️


Previously, I spent a semester studying Engineering at the University of Auckland.


At the intersection of my interdisciplinary passions, I want to help shape inclusive, innovative, and sustainable solutions to real-world problems.
