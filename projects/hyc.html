Howick Youth Council

Graphic design Adobe CC Communications

When?

January - December 2021


Where?

Auckland, New Zealand

A selection of work done during my time as a member of the Howick Youth Council's 2021 Communications team. HYC is an independent, non-partisan youth voice organisation funded by the Howick Local board, striving to make East Auckland a more vibrant place for young people.


I primarily worked as a graphic designer helping to promote the engagement and outreach of Council projects, and contributed to the rebranding of the Council upon its 10th anniversary. This featured a new branding palette with bright and bold colours for greater cohesivity, in combination with a pair of sans-serif font families that are easy on the eyes.


Besides graphic design, I also had the opportunity to explore marketing strategy, event photography and social media management. Seeing my creations being shared by my peers digitally, as well as in print within the community, was an incredibly empowering experience.
