When?

August 2022


Where?

Auckland, New Zealand

Lidy is an upcoming clothing boutique specialising in ball and event wear.


Based upon the initial client request of a light blue and pastel palette, I conceived a flexible identity system that could be scaled and built upon as the business expanded. This includes a set of serif/sans-serif fonts, used in tandem with three unique shapes that can be combined to form a variety of imagery.
