Thoughts and prayers

Fine art Installation Conceptual art

When?

October 2022


Where?

Providence, Rhode Island


Why?

For RISD Foundation Studio: Design (Fall 2022).

27 paper prayers with photoshopped found images, individually cut and folded. Each prayer represents a mass murder by gun in the US this year so far, as defined by the National Gun Violence Archive as a mass shooting of four or more killed.
